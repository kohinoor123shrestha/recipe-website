Masala Dosa

Masala dosa is a popular South Indian dish that consists of a thin, crispy crepe-like pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter, filled with a spiced potato filling. It is typically served with coconut chutney and sambar (a lentil-based vegetable stew). Masala dosa is a staple breakfast item in South India and has gained popularity throughout the country and even internationally.

prep time

40 min

cook time

25 min

serving

2 servings

Tags : vegetarian South-Indian Cuisine crispy chutney sambar

instructions

step 1

Wash the dosa rice and urad dal separately. Soak them in water for about 4-6 hours. Drain the water from the soaked ingredients and grind them to a smooth batter, adding water as needed. Mix the ground batter well, add salt, and let it rest for 30 minutes.

step 2

Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Allow them to splutter.

step 3

Add cumin seeds, asafoetida, and curry leaves. Sauté for a few seconds.

step 4

Add chopped onions and green chilies. Cook until the onions turn translucent. Also add turmeric powder , salt and mix well.

step 5

Add the mashed potatoes and mix everything together. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the flavors blend. Remove from heat.

step 6

Heat a non-stick or cast-iron griddle (tawa) on medium heat. Pour a ladleful of dosa batter onto the center of the griddle and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.

step 7

Drizzle some oil around the edges of the dosa and on the top. Cook the dosa until it turns golden brown and crispy.

step 8

Spread a spoonful of the prepared masala filling on one side of the dosa.

step 9

Fold the dosa in half and gently remove it from the griddle.

step 10

Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar!

ingredients

3 medium sized potatoes,boiled and mashed

1 onion,finely chopped

2 green chillies,finely chopped

½ teaspoon mustard seeds,cumin seeds

a pinch of hing(asafoetida)

1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder,chill powder(optional)

a few curry leaves

2 tablespoon oil

salt (to taste)

tools

Griddle / Tawa

Grinder/Blender

Spatula

chopping board

Knife

Mixing Bowls
