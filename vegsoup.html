VEGETABLE SOUP

Vegetable soup is a dish that typically consists of a flavorful broth or stock simmered with various types of vegetables. It's a versatile and nourishing soup that can be made with a wide variety of vegetables, making it a popular choice among vegetarians and vegans.The specific vegetables used in vegetable soup can vary based on personal preference, seasonal availability, and regional culinary traditions.It can be enjoyed as a light meal on its own or served alongside bread, crackers, or a salad.

prep time

25 min

cook time

15 min

serving

3 servings

Tags : vegetarian low calorie quick soup light meal

instructions

step 1

Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and saut√© until the onion becomes translucent.

step 2

Add the carrots, celery, bell pepper, and zucchini to the pot. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables begin to soften..

step 3

Pour in the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth. Stir in the dried thyme and oregano. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

step 4

Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and let it simmer for about 10-12 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.

step 5

Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if needed.

step 6

Ladle the vegetable soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley, if desired.

step 7

Serve hot and enjoy!

ingredients

4 cups vegetable broth

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 onion diced

2 cloves garlic,minced

2 carrots and 2 celery stalks diced

1 bell pepper diced

1 can diced tomatoes

1 teaspoon dried oregano

Salt and pepper to taste

Fresh parsley,chopped(for garnish, optional)

tools

Chopping board and knife

Large pot or dutch oven

Wooden spoon/spatula

Soup bowls and spoons
