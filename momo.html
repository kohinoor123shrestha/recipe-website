Chicken Mo:Mo

Chicken mo:mo is a type of dumpling that originated in Nepal and is now a popular street food and snack in many South Asian countries. It consists of a filling made from ground chicken mixed with vegetables and spices, wrapped in a thin layer of dough, and steamed or fried. The dish is often served with a spicy dipping sauce made from tomato and chili or sesame and soy sauce. It's a delicious and flavorful snack that can be enjoyed as an appetizer or a main course.

prep time

40 min

cook time

30 min

serving

5 servings

Tags : appetizer Steamed Nepalese Cuisine Dumplings

instructions

step 1

In a mixing bowl, combine the ground chicken, chopped onion, minced garlic, grated ginger, chopped cilantro, soy sauce, oil, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined.

step 2

Take a dumpling wrapper and place a small spoonful of the chicken mixture in the center of the wrapper.

step 3

Moisten the edges of the wrapper with water using your finger. Fold the wrapper in half, creating a semicircle, and pinch the edges together to seal the momo. You can create different pleating designs along the edge for decorative purposes.

step 4

Repeat the process with the remaining wrappers and chicken mixture until you have used all the filling.

step 5

Fill a large pot or wok with water, making sure it's below the level of the steamer. Place the steamer with the momos on top of the pot or wok. Cover with a lid.

step 6

Grease the steamer basket or metal steamer tray with oil or cooking spray to prevent the momos from sticking. Arrange the momos in the steamer, leaving some space between each one to prevent them from sticking together.

step 7

Steam the momos over medium-high heat for about 12-15 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the dumpling wrappers are translucent.Once cooked, carefully remove the steamer from the pot or wok.

step 8

Serve the chicken momos hot with your choice of dipping sauce, such as soy sauce, chili sauce, or tomato-based sauce.

ingredients

250 grams ground chicken

1 small onion, finely chopped

2 garlic cloves, minced

1-inch piece of ginger, grated

2 tablespoons cilantro (coriander leaves), chopped

1 tablespoon soy sauce

1 tablespoon oil

1 teaspoon ground cumin

1 teaspoon ground coriander

1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder

Salt to taste

30-40 round dumpling wrappers

Water for sealing the momo wrappers

tools

Mixing bowl

Spoon or spatula for mixing

Steamer or steaming apparatus

Bamboo steamer basket or metal steamer tray

Large pot or wok with a lid

Plate or tray for arranging the momos

Oil or cooking spray (for greasing the steamer)
