Lemon Garlic Shrimp

Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp is a delicious and flavorful seafood dish. It typically involves sautéing shrimp in a combination of butter, garlic, lemon juice, and various seasonings

prep time

45 min

cook time

35 min

serving

2 servings

Tags : Seafood Tangy Mediterranean
Main Dish Appetizer

instructions

step 1

In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for about 1 minute until the garlic is fragrant.

step 2

Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook the shrimp as they can become rubbery

step 3

Reduce the heat to low. Add the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter to the skillet and allow it to melt. Stir in the lemon juice and zest, and season with salt and black pepper to taste. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, stirring gently to coat the shrimp with the sauce.

step 4

Remove the skillet from the heat. Sprinkle chopped parsley over the shrimp and give it a final toss to incorporate the flavors.

step 5

Serve the lemon garlic butter shrimp hot, garnished with additional parsley and accompanied by lemon wedges for squeezing extra lemon juice if desired. This dish pairs well with steamed rice, pasta, or crusty bread.

ingredients

1 pound (450g) large shrimp, peeled and deveined

4 tablespoons unsalted butter

4 cloves garlic, minced

1 lemon, juice and zest

1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)

Salt, to taste

Freshly ground black pepper

Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Lemon wedges

Tools

Large skillet

Garlic press or mincer

Zester or grater for lemon zest

Wooden spoon or spatula

Kitchen knife & cutting board
