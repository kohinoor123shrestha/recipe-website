Chocolate Brownie

A chocolate brownie is a rich and decadent dessert that is typically made with chocolate, butter, sugar, eggs, and flour. Brownies have a dense and fudgy texture, often with a crackly top and a moist interior. They are beloved by many for their intense chocolate flavor and indulgent nature.

prep time

30 min

cook time

10 min

serving

3 servings

Tags : Dessert sweet treat Chocolate Baked Goods Cocoa

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.

step 2

In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter. Once melted, add the sugar and stir until well combined.

step 3

Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

step 4

In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet mixture, stirring until just combined.If desired, fold in the chocolate chips and chopped nuts.

step 5

Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.

step 6

Bake in the preheated oven for approximately 15 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

step 7

Remove from the oven and allow the brownies to cool completely in the pan before cutting into squares.

step 8

Feel free to customize the recipe by adding ingredients like caramel, marshmallows, or different types of chocolate. Enjoy your homemade chocolate brownies!

ingredients

8 oz pasta (penne or fettuccine)

2 tablespoons butter

2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

1 1/2 cups milk

1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

1/4 teaspoon onion powder

1/4 teaspoon dried oregano

Fresh parsley or basil for garnish (optional)

tools

oven

piping bag/zipping bag

baking sheet

Wooden spoon/ whisk

Mixing Bowls
