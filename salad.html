Vegetable Quinoa Salad

Roasted Vegetable Quinoa Salad is a flavorful and nutritious dish that combines roasted vegetables with protein-packed quinoa. It's a perfect option for a healthy and satisfying vegetarian mea

prep time

15 min

cook time

5 min

serving

2 servings

Tags : Vegetarian healthy Gluten-free Quinoa High in fiber

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil or parchment paper for easy cleanup (optional)

step 2

In a saucepan, bring the vegetable broth or water to a boil. Add the quinoa, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15 minutes or until all the liquid is absorbed. Remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork.

step 3

While the quinoa is cooking, spread the diced eggplant, zucchini, bell peppers, and red onion on the prepared baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat the vegetables evenly.

step 4

Roast the vegetables in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they are tender and lightly browned. Stir the vegetables halfway through cooking to ensure even browning.

step 5

In a mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa and roasted vegetables. Add fresh parsley and lemon juice. Toss well to combine. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.

step 6

Serve the roasted vegetable quinoa salad warm or chilled. You can top it with crumbled feta cheese and toasted pine nuts for extra flavor and texture.

ingredients

1 cup quinoa

2 cups vegetable broth or water

1 small eggplant, zucchini,red /yellow bell pepper ,diced

2 tablespoons olive oil

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 teaspoon dried oregano

1/2 teaspoon dried basil

juice of 1 lemon

1/4 cup fresh parsley,chopped

optional toppings(feta cheese,toasted pine nuts)

tools

Skillet or frying pan

Spatula

Knife for chopping

Cutting board

Grater (if using block cheese)

Mixing bowl
