Banana Pancake

A banana pancake is a type of pancake that is made with mashed ripe bananas added to the batter. The banana gives the pancake a sweet and slightly fruity flavor and also makes it moist and fluffy.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : brunch healthy quick meal fluffy easy recipe

instructions

step 1

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.

step 2

In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, mashed bananas, and melted butter or oil.

step 3

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and whisk until just combined. Do not overmix - the batter should be lumpy.

step 4

Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Spoon about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.

step 5

Cook the pancakes for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through.

step 6

Serve the pancakes warm with your desired toppings!

ingredients

1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder

1 1/4 cups milk

1 large egg

2 ripe bananas, mashed

1 large egg

1 tablespoon soy sauce

Optional toppings: sliced bananas, maple syrup, whipped cream

tools

Mixing Bowl

Whisk/Fork

Spatula

Frying pan /Griddle
