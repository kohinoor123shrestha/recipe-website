Butter Chicken

Butter chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a popular Indian dish that originated in the Punjab region of India. It is a flavorful and creamy dish made with marinated and grilled chicken pieces cooked in a rich tomato-based gravy. The chicken is typically marinated in a mixture of yogurt, spices, and lemon juice, which helps to tenderize the meat and infuse it with flavors. The marinated chicken is then cooked in a tandoor (traditional clay oven) or grilled until it is slightly charred and cooked through.

prep time

50 min

cook time

40 min

serving

3 servings

Tags : Indian cuisine Tandoori chicken Main course Creamy

instructions

step 1

In a large bowl, whisk yogurt, ginger, garlic, garam masala, chili powder, cumin, turmeric, and 2 1/2 teaspoons salt. Stir in chicken and refrigerate at least 1 hour or up to 24.

step 2

In a large high-sided skillet over medium-heat heat, heat oil. Add chicken to pan in an even layer and cook, undisturbed, until golden brown on bottom side, 8 to 10 minutes. Using a slotted spoon, transfer chicken to a bowl.

step 3

Without wiping out pan, combine ghee and onions; season with 1 teaspoon salt. Cook, stirring often, until golden brown, 8 to 10 minutes

step 4

Add ginger, garlic, garam masala, cumin, and Kashmiri powder and cook, stirring, until fragrant, 30 seconds to 1 minute. Pour in tomatoes and granulated sugar and bring to a simmer.

step 5

Cook, stirring occasionally, until sauce thickens and deepens in color, 15 to 20 minutes.

step 6

Transfer sauce to a blender, add cashews, and blend on high speed until very smooth. Pour sauce back into pan and stir in cream and chicken

step 7

Simmer until sauce is thickened and bubbling and chicken is cooked through, 5 to 8 minutes.

step 8

Divide chicken among plates. Top with cilantro. Serve with rice and naan alongside.

ingredients

1/2 c. plain or Greek yogurt

1 piece ginger, peeled and finely chopped or grated

2 cloves garlic, finely chopped or grated

2 lb. boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1 1/2" cubes

1 tsp. chili powder, ground cumin ,ground turmeric

2 tsp. garam masala

2 tbsp. ghee or unsalted butter

1/2 tsp. Indian Kashmiri powder or cayenne pepper

1/4 c. unsalted roasted cashews

1/2 c. heavy cream

1 (28-oz.) can crushed tomatoes

2 tbsp. neutral oil

Chopped cilantro, steamed rice, and naan, for serving

tools

skillet/saucepan

Grinder/Blender

kitchen knife & cutting board

grill/tandoor(clay oven)
