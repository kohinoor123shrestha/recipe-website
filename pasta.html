White Sauce Pasta

White sauce pasta, also known as alfredo pasta, is a popular Italian dish made with a creamy white sauce, typically made from butter, flour, and milk or cream. The sauce is seasoned with garlic, onion, and herbs such as oregano or parsley, and often includes grated parmesan cheese. The pasta is typically cooked until al dente and then mixed with the white sauce. White sauce pasta is a simple and comforting dish that can be customized with various types of pasta, vegetables, and proteins such as chicken or shrimp. It's a favorite among pasta lovers and can be found on many Italian restaurant menus.

prep time

30 min

cook time

10 min

serving

2 servings

Tags : Italian cuisine Creamy dinner main dish white sauce

instructions

step 1

Cook the pasta according to the package instructions .

step 2

In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.Add all-purpose flour to the melted butter and stir continuously until the mixture turns golden brown, about 2-3 minutes.

step 3

Gradually add the milk while stirring continuously. Mix until the sauce thickens and becomes smooth.

step 4

Add the garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, salt, and pepper to the sauce. Mix well.

step 5

Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and mix until the pasta is coated evenly with the sauce.

step 6

Sprinkle grated parmesan cheese on top of the pasta and mix well.Garnish with fresh parsley or basil, if desired.

step 7

Serve your white sauce pasta hot and enjoy!

ingredients

8 oz pasta (penne or fettuccine)

2 tablespoons butter

2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

1 1/2 cups milk

1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

1/4 teaspoon onion powder

1/4 teaspoon dried oregano

Fresh parsley or basil for garnish (optional)

tools

Sauce pan

Colander

Grater

Wooden spoon/ Spatula
