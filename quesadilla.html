QUESADILLA

A quesadilla is a Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla filled with cheese and other ingredients, then folded in half and toasted until the cheese melts. It can be enjoyed as a snack, appetizer, or even a main course when accompanied by toppings like salsa, guacamole, or sour cream.

prep time

45 min

cook time

25 min

serving

1 servings

Tags : Mexican cuisine Melted cheese Appetizer Tortilla

instructions

step 1

In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat olive oil. Add peppers and onion and season with salt and pepper.

step 2

Cook until soft for approx. 5 minutes. then, transfer to a plate.

step 3

Heat remaining tablespoon vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Season chicken with spices, salt, and pepper and cook, stirring occasionally, until golden and cooked through. Then,transfer to a plate.

step 4

Add 1 flour tortilla to skillet and top half of the tortilla with a heavy sprinkling of both cheeses, cooked chicken mixture, pepper-onion mixture, a few slices of avocado, and green onions.

step 5

Fold the other half of the tortilla over and cook, flipping once, until golden. Repeat to make 4 quesadillas.

step 6

Slice into wedges and serve with sour cream

ingredients

1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil

2 bell peppers, thinly sliced

1/2 onion, thinly sliced

Kosher salt

1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts, sliced into strips

1/2 tsp. chili powder,dried oregano

4 medium flour tortillas

2 c. shredded Monterey jack,shredded cheddar

2 scallions, thinly sliced

tools

Frying Pan or Skillet

spatula

knife

Grater
